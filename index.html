About

Hello! My name is Hyogi Sim, and I am a HPC Systems Engineer in the Technology Integration Group under National Center for Computational Sciences (NCCS) at Oak Ridge National Laboratory. I am designing and developing softwares for file and storage systems that serve large-scale HPC systems such as Titan and Summit.

I am also a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech. I work with Dr. Ali R. Butt, who leads the Distributed Systems and Storage Laboratory. My research interest primarily lies in large-scale storage systems and scientific data management, and I am also interested in general topics in file systems and database management systems.
