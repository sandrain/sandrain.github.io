About

Hello! My name is Hyogi Sim. I am a HPC Systems Engineer in the Technology Integration Group under National Center for Computational Sciences (NCCS) at Oak Ridge National Laboratory. I design and develop softwares for file and storage systems that serve large-scale HPC systems like Titan and Summit.

I am also a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech. I work with Dr. Ali R. Butt, who leads the Distributed Systems and Storage Laboratory (DSSL). My research interest primarily lies in large-scale storage systems and scientific data management. I am also interested in general topics in file systems and database management systems.
